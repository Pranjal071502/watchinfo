About

Citizen Watch Co., Ltd., established in 1930, is a renowned Japanese watchmaker celebrated for its innovation and craftsmanship. The company originated from the Shokosha Watch Research Institute, founded in 1918, and has since evolved into a global brand operating in over 140 markets worldwide.

Philosophy and Technology

Citizen's guiding philosophy, "Better Starts Now," reflects its commitment to continuous improvement and innovation. The company is a true manufacture, handling all aspects of watch production in-house, from crafting individual components to final assembly. This vertical integration ensures high-quality standards and allows for the development of proprietary technologies.

One of Citizen's most notable innovations is the Eco-Drive technology, which powers watches using light, eliminating the need for battery replacements. This environmentally friendly approach aligns with the company's sustainability initiatives.

Product Lines and Models

  • Promaster Diver: A professional-grade diving watch known for its durability and water resistance, suitable for underwater adventures.
  • Eco-Drive One: The world's thinnest light-powered watch, showcasing Citizen's technological prowess and sleek design.
  • ATTESA HAKUTO-R Collaboration Model: A limited-edition watch inspired by lunar exploration, featuring advanced materials and satellite timekeeping.
  • Series 8 880 Mechanical Model: A mechanical watch combining traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics, offering precision and style.
  • Citizen L Bianca: An elegant women's watch with a mother-of-pearl dial and Eco-Drive technology, blending luxury with sustainability.

Pricing

Citizen's watches are available at various price points to accommodate different budgets. For instance, the Citizen L Bianca models are priced around $319, offering affordable luxury.

Sustainability Initiatives

Citizen is dedicated to sustainability, utilizing materials like lab-grown diamonds and FSC-certified paper. The company is a member of the Leather Working Group (LWG) and participates in initiatives such as "1% for the Planet," contributing a portion of sales to environmental causes.

Global Presence

With a presence in over 140 countries, Citizen has established itself as a global leader in the watch industry, offering timepieces that blend tradition, innovation, and sustainability.
